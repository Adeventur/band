SHALLIPOPI

Crown Uzama, known professionally as Shallipopi, is a Nigerian rapper, singer and songwriter. He gained initial recognition with the release of "Obapluto" in 2023. Shallipopi was signed to Dvpper Music Distribution, a Nigerian music record distribution founded by "Damillola Akinwunmi".
